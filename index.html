Privacy Policy

Wakezy Alarm  ·  Last updated: June 23, 2026

Short version: Wakezy Alarm does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Overview

Wakezy Alarm ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile alarm application developed for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we handle and how.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Wakezy Alarm does not collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers
  • Health or fitness data

3. Data Stored Locally

All alarm settings, preferences, and app data are stored exclusively on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults / local database). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us.

4. Third-Party Services

Wakezy Alarm does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries. No data is shared with third parties.

5. Permissions

The app may request the following iOS permissions solely to deliver core functionality:

  • Notifications – to trigger alarm alerts at the scheduled time.
  • Background App Refresh – to ensure alarms fire reliably even when the app is in the background.

These permissions are used only on-device and do not transmit any data externally.

6. Children's Privacy

Wakezy Alarm does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Because we collect no data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
